We at TDR strongly believe in the power of teamwork, collaboration, innovation, and data-driven community work. We invite you to contribute and make a significant impact on our collective change in the society:

Share your Data or Insights

Contribute any datasets you may have related to our community. Or dive deep into our open datasets, identify patterns or insights, and send your analysis to our email at info@datafortigray.org. Your datasets will be available in our repository, and your work will be featured in the TDR Journal, advancing discoveries that enhance our collective understanding.

Support Our Mission and Vision

Our work encompasses various elements and we would greatly appreciate any financial contribution towards our initiative to keep our work going. You can help fund data-driven community projects for better service delivery and ensure our history has a living memory through these valuable projects. Your engagement—whether through intellectual exploration or financial support—brings us closer to documenting and preserving our collective history.

Let’s build this legacy together: Data for Tigray, knowledge for generations!
