Title

Data for Tigray, Knowledge for Generations


Introduction

The Tigray Data Repository is more than a repository; it’s a movement toward the documentation, preservation, and sharing with the world of the rich history, culture, and experiences of Tigray and its diaspora. The TDR was created out of necessity. It is an initiative that focuses on building a data-based platform where data is transformed into knowledge and actionable insights. This platform also transforms history into a tool to advocate for change and raise awareness about Tigray-related issues. It seeks to empower future generations with the tools to understand their past, shape their present, and build their future.


Vision

It is the main protector of data about Tigray, ensuring it will be preserved and available for future generations. At the same time, it raises awareness and promotes this cause around the world.


Mission

TDR is committed to documenting, preserving, and making available data related to Tigray’s history, culture, and socio-political contexts. Through making this data available to everyone, we are creating opportunities for researchers, educators, and advocates all over the world. They can use this information to take action in areas like research, education, innovation, and justice.


Core Values

  • Integrity: Demonstrating precision and ethical behavior in how we collect and manage data.
  • Collaboration: Working alongside partners across the world to enhance TDR’s resources and expand our outreach.
  • Empowerment: Providing Tigrayans and the worldwide community with the knowledge they need for informed choices.
  • Accountability: Using data helps us to reveal truths, fight against misinformation, and promote justice.

Objectives

  • Open, reliable and centralized datasets about Tigray.
  • Make note of significant occurrences like the Tigray Genocide in detail and with accuracy.
  • Help to maintain and enhance the involvement of Tigrayan diaspora individuals in international awareness.
  • Making it easier for people to understand and use data by providing interactive dashboards, holding workshops, and creating publications.
  • Integrate TDR resources in education and policy by collaborating with government agencies, stakeholders and international organizations.

Methodology

TDR employs diverse data collection and analysis methods, including historical records, surveys, digital campaigns, and reports from government agencies, stakeholders, and other organizations. All data is structured, anonymized, and checked before use to be used both properly and with effect.

Impact:

The TDR will be a living memory of the history and resilience of Tigray, passed on through generations for the purpose of awareness, research, and education. It forms a foundation for justice, an instrument of accountability, and serves as a sign of hope.

Call to Action:

Join us in this great undertaking: donate your data, volunteer skills, or financial support to our cause. Together, we can make sure Tigray’s story will never be forgotten and that its future is built on a basis of truth and resilience.

Contact Information:

Explore, contribute, and connect: info@datagray.org.

The Tigray Data Repository serves an important role beyond data preservation; it safeguards not only data but also a community, its culture, and its heritage. Together, let’s unite in the cause: Empowering Tigray with data, great knowledge for future generations!
