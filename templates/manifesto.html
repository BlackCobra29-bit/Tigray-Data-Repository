Title:

Building the Future Through Our Stories


Introduction:

The Tigray Data Repository is more than just an archive; it is a movement born out of necessity, dedicated to documenting, preserving, and sharing the rich history, culture, and experiences of Tigrayans with the world. Our platform is an essential tool for data-based knowledge that can be used to advocate for change and raise awareness on Tigray-related issues. We seek to empower future generations with the tools to understand their past, shape their present, and build their future.


Vision:

At TDR, we envision a future where the rich history, culture, and experiences of the Tigrayan communities are methodically preserved and readily accessible to both the community and future generations.


Mission:

We are dedicated to documenting, preserving, and making data related to Tigray’s history, culture, and economic and socio-political contexts widely accessible. As such, we strive to create a comprehensive archive that provides invaluable resources for researchers, educators, and advocates.


Core Values:

  • Integrity: We are committed to ethical data collection and management practices.
  • Collaboration: We work alongside stakeholders, committed individuals, and other local and global partners to enhance TDR’s resources and expand our outreach and impact.
  • Empowerment: Our focus is to provide Tigrayans and the global community with the data they need to make informed choices about matters related to Tigray and its community.
  • Accountability: Through the data we collect and archive, along with the insights shared in our journal, we strive to tell truths, combat misinformation, and promote justice and accountability.

Objectives:

  • To create and maintain open and reliable datasets on Tigray and its Diaspora.
  • Provide detailed and accurate records of critical occurrences, such as the Tigray Genocide, to preserve history and promote accountability.
  • To help maintain and enhance the active participation of the Tigrayan Diaspora in international advocacy and awareness efforts related to their communities.
  • To utilize modern technologies to gather real-time data and insights about Tigray and its Diaspora.
  • Making it easier for people to understand and use data by providing interactive dashboards, holding workshops, and creating publications.
  • Integrate TDR resources in education and policy by collaborating with government agencies, stakeholders and international organizations.

Methodology:

At TDR, we employ various data collection and analysis methods, including historical records, surveys, digital campaigns, and reports from government and non-governmental agencies, as well as other organizations and dedicated individuals. All our data is structured, anonymized, and thoroughly checked before use to ensure proper and practical application.

Impact

The Tigray Data Repository (TDR) will serve as a living memory of Tigray’s history and resilience, preserving its data and sharing knowledge for future generations. In this way, we will help lay the groundwork for justice, act as an instrument of accountability, and symbolize hope for the future.

Call to Action: Join us in Shaping the Future

Given the significant nature of this initiative, it necessitates strong institutional commitments, resources, and collaborative efforts. We strongly believe that our collective efforts and individual commitments allow us to achieve far greater outcomes. Therefore, we would be honored to have you as part of our ecosystem in achieving our goals for a better Tigray! You can help us achieve this by sharing any datasets, volunteering your skills, or providing financial support to our cause. Together, we can ensure that Tigray’s story is preserved and its future is built on truth and resilience!

Contact Information:

Explore, contribute, and connect: info@datafortigray.org.


Closing:

The Tigray Data Repository is a beacon of resilience and a hope for a better, informed future. As guardians of the data of Tigray and its people, we relish in preserving and sharing the history and experience of Tigrayans to empower the next generation. Together, we can stand united in our commitment to honor the past, enrich the present, and illuminate a brighter future for Tigray.
